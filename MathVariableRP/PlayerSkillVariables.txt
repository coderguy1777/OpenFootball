Parameters for player variables:
--------------------------------------
n_downs = numbers of total downs

(Delta)y_gained = yards gained by player

(Delta)y_loss = yards lost by player

I_n = Number of interceptions by player.

I_incn = Number of failed interceptions by player

I_cn = Number of interceptions completed by the player

(Delta)wk_l = # of weeks left in the season

C_wk = current week in season

T_wk = weeks in season

C_p = # of completed passes by player

Inc_p = # of incomplete passes by the player

2Pm = # of two point conversions made by player in question.
